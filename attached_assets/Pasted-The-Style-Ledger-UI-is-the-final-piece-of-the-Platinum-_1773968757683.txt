2. The "Override" Control
The most important part of the Style Ledger is the Manual Override. If the AI is being "too aggressive" with geo-rejection (e.g., rejecting "Cape Cod" when that's actually a valid semantic region), you can add a "White Label" exception.

Logic: The SEOPolicy checks the Ledger for "Whitelisted Phrases" before applying the isBareLocation rejection.

Result: You get total control over the "Prompt Drift" without ever touching the code again.
