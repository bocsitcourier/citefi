Here’s the detailed implementation plan you can hand off to your developer:

🎧 AI Conversational Podcast Generator (Gemini + TTS Module)
🔹 Goal

Transform each AI-generated article into a podcast episode featuring two realistic voices (one male, one female) who casually discuss the article’s content.
Each podcast is saved, playable at the top of the article page, and added to the media library for reuse, download, or social sharing.

🧩 System Architecture

1. Trigger

When a new article is completed, trigger an async “Generate Podcast” job.

Or allow manual “🎙 Create Podcast” button in the editor.

2. Steps in the Pipeline

Summarization:
Gemini summarizes the article into 5–8 main talking points.

Prompt:

“Summarize this article into 5 conversational talking points for a two-host podcast. Keep tone friendly, informative, and natural.”

Script Generation:
Gemini generates a two-speaker dialogue (Host 1 = Female, Host 2 = Male).

Prompt example:

“Write a natural, 3–5 minute conversational script between two hosts (one female, one male) discussing this article. Include introductions, reactions, transitions, and conclusions.”

Voice Synthesis:

Use Gemini TTS or Google Cloud Text-to-Speech with SSML for tone and pacing.

Assign:

voice_female = en-US-Wavenet-F

voice_male = en-US-Wavenet-D

Audio Mixing:

Merge both audio streams with background music (optional).

Normalize volume (−3 LUFS target).

Output format: .mp3 (128 kbps).

Storage & Linking:

Store audio file in /media/podcasts/.

Add metadata to articles table:

podcast_url TEXT,
podcast_duration INT,
podcast_generated_at TIMESTAMP,
podcast_status ENUM('pending','processing','ready','failed')


UI Integration:

Add embedded player at top of article:

<audio controls preload="none" src="{{article.podcast_url}}"></audio>


Include “🎧 Listen to this article” caption.

🧠 Smart AI Prompting

Gemini prompt should consider:

Article’s tone and industry (business, lifestyle, tech, etc.).

Desired mood (friendly, confident, inspiring).

Duration (auto-scaled by article length).
Example:

Create a conversational 2-host podcast script summarizing this business article.
Duration: ~4 minutes. Tone: friendly, insightful. Include intro and outro.

🔄 Async Processing

Use background worker (e.g., Node Queue / BullMQ / Cloud Tasks).

Each article → generatePodcastJob(article_id) with retry logic.

Return status_url to frontend for live progress updates.
